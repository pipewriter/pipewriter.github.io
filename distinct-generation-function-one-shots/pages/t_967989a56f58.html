◈ COALESCENT DRIFT FIELD

Every location carries a genome sampled from a fractional-Brownian vector field. Spatial distance behaves like evolutionary divergence time: neighbors share their low-frequency genes (similar, not identical); distant places have drifted across every scale (utterly foreign).

Topography, palette, and life are all derived from the local genome. Stateless · deterministic · O(1) per point.
