The Way Homea mnemonic trail · the method of loci

Before maps, before writing, before paper — a human being held an entire journey in memory, or did not make it home. This valley is built the way memory wants it: ten landmarks, in order, each unlike any other.

Walk the trail once (about a minute). Name each landmark as you pass it. Follow the smoke. When you arrive, try to walk it again with your eyes closed.

Now the real walk begins — the one in your head. Close your eyes and pass them in order:

  1. 🗿 The Twin Watchers — two stones guard the way in
  2. 🍎 The Crooked Elder — the bent tree heavy with red fruit
  3. 💧 The Singing Ford — stepping stones across cold water
  4. 💀 The Skull Stake — a warning where ravens circle
  5. ✨ Glowmoss Hollow — blue light beneath dark pines
  6. 🌉 The Fallen Giant — one log over the ravine
  7. 🪨 The Three Cairns — stacked by every traveler before you
  8. 🦴 The Bone Chimes — white birches that rattle in the wind
  9. ✋ The Painted Wall — the hands of those who walked first
  10. 🔥 The Home Fire — the smoke you followed

Place a memory at each locus and it will wait there for you.
The trail never changes. It is the same valley every time you return.
