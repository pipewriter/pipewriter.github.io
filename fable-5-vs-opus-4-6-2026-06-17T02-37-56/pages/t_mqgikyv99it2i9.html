The Trail Home

Dusk is falling. You are far from the fire, and there is no map, no paper, no GPS — only the land itself. Walk home the way the first people did: toward the setting sun, between the Twin Peaks, reading one landmark after the next.

Ten landmarks mark the way. Each has a shape, a color, a sound, a turn of the path. When you reach the fire, try to walk the trail again — with your eyes closed.
