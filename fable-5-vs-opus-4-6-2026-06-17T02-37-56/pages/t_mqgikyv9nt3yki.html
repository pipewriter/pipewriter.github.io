THE WAY HOME

A one-minute trail with seven landmarks, each one too strange to forget. Walk it the way your ancestors did — no map, no compass, no paper.

The old skills: keep the low sun at your back-left going out — coming home it will burn ahead of you. Count the marks, one to seven, gate to fire. Glance back now and then — the trail home wears a different face.
