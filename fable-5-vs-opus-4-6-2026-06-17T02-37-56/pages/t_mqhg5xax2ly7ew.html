THE WAY HOME

Dusk gathers. Like every walker before maps, before writing, before paper itself, you must carry the trail in your mind. Eight signs mark the way home. Notice them. Name them aloud. The land is the map.

YOU ARE HOME BEFORE DARK

    Mind-palace tip: close your eyes and walk the trail backwards, from the fire to the fire. Then place one thing you must remember at each of the eight signs — the trail will keep them for you.
