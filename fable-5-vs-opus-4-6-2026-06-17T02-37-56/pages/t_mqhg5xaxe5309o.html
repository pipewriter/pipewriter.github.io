THE WAY HOME

a memory trail

Before maps, before writing — before paper itself — the way home lived in the mind. A hunter read the land like a story: the tusks, then the red hand, then the crooked tree…

Walk this trail once, with attention. Ten landmarks, in order, each stranger than the last. The sun will sink as you go. Be home before dark.

No map. No compass. Just the land, and you.
