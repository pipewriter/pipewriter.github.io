About Me

Hi, I'm Parker, a graphics engineer who is focused on making powerful tools to help people remember better. I moved out to California after listening to Peter Thiel explain how my personal heroes were able to change the world with new technologies by being non-imitative.

I believe that in the future, many of the current computer usage methods will give way to an environment that is more human-brain ergonomic. The brain is adapted to memorizing a 3D environment, sound, song, smell, or through conversational cues -- and not through a giant chronologically sorted list. I intend to invent that project, or join a company that has that mission.
