THE EMBER ROAD

Night has fallen on the volcano. You are far from the hearth, and there is no map, no star you trust, no voice to call. There is only the trail — and the things along it your mind must never let you forget.

Walk it once. Burn each landmark into memory. Find your way home before the fires die.

The road takes about a minute. Stay on the glowing stones.

YOU ARE HOME

Now close your eyes. Can you walk the Ember Road again, in your mind, in order?
