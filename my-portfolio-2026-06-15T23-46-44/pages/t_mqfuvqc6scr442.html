The Way Home

You are far from the fire, and night is coming. There is no map, no star to follow yet — only the trail and the things along it that the old ones left, or that the mountain made. Remember them. Each is a marker on the only road home.

Walk the trail. Pass beneath the red arch, follow the water's voice, honor the bear, cross the fallen giant — and let the fire welcome you. About one minute, if you do not linger.
