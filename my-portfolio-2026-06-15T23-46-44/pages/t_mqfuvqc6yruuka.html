The Drowned Trail Home

The sun is sinking and the tide is turning. You are far out along the reef, and you must follow the old path home before the dark water closes in — no map, no star, only the things you have learned to remember.

Walk past the eight markers your people have always used. Notice each one. Burn its shape, its colour, its sound into your mind. One day you will need to find your way back in the black.

▸ Move: W A S D  ·  Look: Mouse  ·  Follow the glowing pearls. The walk takes about one minute.

You Are Home.

You followed the kelp gate, the drowned anchor, the ring of lights, the red arch, the great ribs, the breathing clams, and the silver storm of fish — and the water brought you home.

Close your eyes. Can you still walk it backward?
