Depth Partitioning — The Vertical City

You stand on one floor of one tower. Only that floor is rendered near you; the city and the mountains live in their own frustums far beyond.

Press Q/E to drop or climb a floor — instant, deterministic: the same floor always rebuilds the same.

Walk to a window, aim the crosshair at another tower — a floor-segment lights up — and click to teleport onto that exact floor.
